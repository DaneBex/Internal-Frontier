  ### A rational God and a searching mind

While looking at the heavens above on January 7, 1610. Galileo Galilei discovered the moons of Jupiter. This was a monumental discovery that changed the way we viewed the 
universe. It was the first time we had seen celestial bodies orbiting something other than Earth. This discovery led to a shift in our understanding of the universe and our 
place in it. It was a pivotal moment in the history of science and astronomy.
